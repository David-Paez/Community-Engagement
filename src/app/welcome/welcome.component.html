IDH 3034: Community Engagement Project

Proposal

Our idea is to create an interactive website experience in which we explore traditional instruments from Africa and Asia. The website features different instruments from various countries, and you can hover over the instruments to play audio samples. Due to its web presence, this project is very easily shareable with the class and the FIU community through a simple URL.

About

Leila Adaza and David Paez are currently juniors majoring in Computer Science. Together, they created this website as a final project for the Aural Engagement course, taught by Professor McDaniel and Professor Calloway in the FIU Honors College.
